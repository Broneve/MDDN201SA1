Where to find the internet

Powerlines

We can see powerlines almost everywhere we go. They connect power and internet through wires. Some areas have put these wires undergrond to protect them from the weather and to make areas look nicer. these lines allow us to have routers and personal data in many different areas. Areas with limite or no powerlines may not have access to the internet.

Routers

We have wireless routers in our home to provide us with fast internet. We are also able to connect some of our devices with an ethernet cable to provide us with faster uninterupted connection.

Mobilephones

We can connect our smart devices to any wifi connection we have the password to and we are able to buy personal data. This is all done wirelessly. This personal data allows us to take the internet withus wherever we go cor limitless communication to anyone arround the world with an internt connection.

Cars

Many cars today are able to connect to the internet. Some cars have a screen where the radio used to be that has acces to GPS, bluetooth, gthe internet via google, the timezone and so much more. There are other cars however in which they have been designed for the internet toallow the user to control mearly all aspects of their car with ther own device or the iPad decive in the car. .
